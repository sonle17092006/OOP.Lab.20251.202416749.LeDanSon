Question 2:
Encapsulation is wrapping data (attributes) and methods into a single unit and restricting direct access to them.
We need it to protect data integrity and hide implementation details.
Two techniques I used:
1. Declare all attributes private
2. Provide public getter/setter methods (and sometimes package-private or protected when needed)
